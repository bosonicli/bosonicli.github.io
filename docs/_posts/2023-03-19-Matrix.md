# Diagonalization / Inverse

$$
\begin{aligned}
    A^{-1} &= \frac{1}{det(A)} cof(A)   \\
    cof(A)_{ij} & \equiv (-)^{i+j} det(A_{ji})  \\
    \begin{pmatrix}
        a \quad b \\
        c \quad d
    \end{pmatrix}
    ^{-1} &=
    \frac{1}{ad-bc}
    \begin{pmatrix}
        d \quad & -c  \\
        -b \quad & a
    \end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$
