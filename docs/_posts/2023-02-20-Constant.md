# Planck Units

\\( c , G , \hbar , k_{B} \\)

$$
\begin{aligned}
    \ell_{P} &= \sqrt{ \frac{ \hbar G }{c^3} }  \\
    &= 1.616 * 10^{-35} m   \\
    t_{P} &= \frac{\ell_{P}}{c} \\
    &= 5.391 * 10^{-44} s   \\
    T_{P} &= \frac{ \hbar c }{ \ell_{P} k_{B}}  \\
    &= \sqrt{ \frac{ \hbar c^5 }{ G k_{B}^2 } } \\
    &= 1.417 * 10^{32} K   \\
    m_{P} &= \frac{ \hbar c }{ \ell_{P} c^2 } \\
    &= \sqrt{\frac{ \hbar c }{G}}
\end{aligned}
$$
